# Research Warrant
Research Warrant is a creative agency focused on reviving dormant catalog assets through data-driven content distribution and audience intelligence.

## Company Overview
Research Warrant positions itself as a partner for:
- Labels
- Studios
- Streaming platforms
- Podcasts
- TV networks
- Indie creators
- Asset managers

Its core value proposition is turning underused content libraries into measurable demand by identifying real audience signals at scale.

## What the Company Does
Research Warrant operates with a three-part model:

1. **Discovery**  
   Tests 100+ catalog assets across 2,000+ social accounts to identify audience demand.

2. **Distribution**  
   Uses proprietary posting infrastructure to deploy content across platforms at scale.

3. **Intelligence**  
   Provides real-time analytics that connect content virality to streaming consumption and marketing performance.

## Services Included in Partnerships
- Premium content creation (supported by proprietary technology and custom datasets)
- Multi-platform distribution
- Real-time reporting dashboard
